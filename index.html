Dead Poets'
Pen Pal

— a letter from history, addressed to you —

Somewhere in the candle-smoke of the archives, a letter-writer has been waiting on you. Whisper your full name. We will rifle through the registry, ask you a few intimate questions, and find your epistolary twin.

Your full name, please
A few intimate queries

Dipping the nib in iron-gall ink…

Your epistolary twin is

— postmarked in the quiet hours —
